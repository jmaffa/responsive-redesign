Meet Oscar

Chef and owner Oscar Garcia, comes to Boxborough after 20 years of working on the Boston fine dining scene. The Guatamalan-born chef secured this Boxborough/Acton suburban location,  to build a professional kitchen where none existed. The barnlike structure with high ceilings and a casual concept shares spaces with an ice cream shop and sits in throwing distance to an ice rink.

Flavorful Mexican food that is fresh and homemade by Oscar, who is in the kitchen daily. Family-friendly, fast-casual Mexican, for a terrific value!

“Food is my passion - my art is creating a delicious and healthy Mexican grill menu that comforts and makes you want more and more!”
